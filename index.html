Legal Information

Data Deletion Request

If you would like to request deletion of your personal data from GoVista, please follow these steps:

  1. Open the GoVista app
  2. Go to Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Data Deletion
  3. Follow the instructions to submit your request
  4. Alternatively, contact us at: support@govista.app

We will process your request within 30 days as required by data protection regulations.

Account Deletion Request

To permanently delete your GoVista account:

  1. Open the GoVista app
  2. Navigate to Profile ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Account
  3. Select "Delete Account" and confirm your choice
  4. All your data, including favorites and preferences, will be permanently removed

Note: This action cannot be undone.
